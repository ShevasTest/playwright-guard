# Code of Conduct

Playwright Guard is an open technical community. Participation must be respectful, constructive, and safe.

## Expected behavior

- Discuss ideas and code without attacking people.
- Assume good intent while remaining precise about technical risks.
- Welcome contributors with different experience levels and backgrounds.
- Give actionable feedback and acknowledge corrections.
- Respect privacy and do not publish another person's private information.

## Unacceptable behavior

Harassment, discrimination, threats, sexualized attention, deliberate intimidation, sustained disruption, and retaliation are not accepted in project spaces.

## Enforcement

Report conduct concerns privately to the maintainer through the contact method in [SECURITY.md](SECURITY.md), clearly marking the message as a conduct report. Reports will be reviewed confidentially and proportionately. Maintainers may edit or remove content, issue a warning, temporarily restrict participation, or permanently ban a participant.

This policy applies to repositories, issues, pull requests, discussions, and other spaces where someone represents the project.
